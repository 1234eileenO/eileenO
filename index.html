YouNote

YouNote is a timestamp inserter and note-taking app for YouTube videos. Users can share YouTube videos to YouNote to make notes under each video and denote the part that they want to play in the future by inserting a timestamp. Videos then can be searched for their content and title information. It is an android application written in Java.

Glide, REST API, SQLite, RecyclerView, SharedPreferences and more were used. Adobe XD was used for the UI design.

MoodDiary

MoodDiary is a mood-tracking android app, written in Java. It allows users to record their daily mood and view statistics reports regarding their entries. Users can also set passwords for their privacy.

RecyclerView, SharedPreferences, ConstraintView, RelativeView, and more were used. Adobe XD was used for the UI design

capMerger

capMerger is an image processing GUI, written in Python. Taking and processing multiple screenshots can be time-consuming. This GUI automates taking screenshots, saving them, resizing images, and merging images in a much simpler way.

PIL, tkinter and more were used. Figma was used for the UI design.
